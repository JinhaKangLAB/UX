ChromaBlend

  • ChromaBlend: Nature's Palette for Your Face (2021)
  • Materials: Color detecting sensor, led, arduino nano, and wires
  • Discover the fusion of technology and nature with ChromaBlend,
    the innovative wearable device that transforms your makeup routine.
    ChromaBlend captures the vibrant colors from your surroundings
    and seamlessly applies them to your face, creating a unique and dynamic look
    inspired by the beauty of nature.
    Whether it's the soft hues of a sunset, the vivid shades of a blooming flower,
    or the serene tones of a forest,
    ChromaBlend ensures your makeup is always in harmony with the world around you.
    Embrace the art of natural beauty and let ChromaBlend bring the colors of nature to your face.


  • This project originated from a single question that popped into my head as I was strolling down the street,
    mesmerized by the vibrant hues of nature.
    I pondered, "What if I could infuse the color of nature into my make-up directly? How can I extend nature into my body?" .
