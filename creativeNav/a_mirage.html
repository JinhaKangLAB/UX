Mirage

  • 2023 - Present

  • A relationship with a particular person can resemble a mirage in the desert.
    It looks like perfect and beautiful water from a certain distance,
    but it is just an illusion that does not exist from the beginning.
    Although every time We realize it is just an illusion,
    We immediately forget about it and get fooled by it again,
    and repeat the same mistake whenever We find that perfect mirage.

    This piece is interactive depending on audience's distance.
