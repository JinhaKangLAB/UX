Life on the Run


Written by Dillon Foster

This Interactive webpage created to interact with the audience not only at the exhibition place, but also in the virtual world.
The website is telling the story by looping and evoking curiosity from the audience.
The audience can make the loop stop to read, but the entire loop is infinitely going on and on like the story of 'Life on the Run'.
