Life on the Run

Client Ask: Develop a website with interactive features designed to engage exhibition attendees and spark their curiosity, motivating them to delve deeper into the written content.

This interactive webpage is designed to engage with the audience, not only at the exhibition venue
but also in the virtual world.
The website conveys a narrative through a continuous loop, aiming to pique the audience's curiosity.
Visitors have the option to pause the loop for reading, although the narrative itself continues endlessly, much like the story of 'Life on the Run.'

Role: Art Director
Web Design | User Flow | Web Development
Tools: Figma, HTML, CSS
